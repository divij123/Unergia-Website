About Us

For India to grow in leaps and bounds, it is imperative that it invests in cleaner sources of energy. Unergia is helping India realise its potential in unlocking its renewable capacity. The Government set a target of generating 40 GW of renewable energy through rooftops by 2022, out of which only 2 GW has been generated up until now. The reason for the low uptake is high initial cost of investment and lack of sound financing options.

The existing energy infrastructure is centered on a few companies that wield power over electricity usage. This power leads to indiscriminate hike in energy prices at regular intervals. Unergia is an investment platform for solar projects, based on crowdfunding. We shall help rooftop providers fund their solar rooftop projects, with all the miscellaneous costs. In turn, investors of these projects will be contracted to receive financial rewards at the rate of 10-15% per year on their investment, over the life of the solar project. We are committed to ensure that everybody wins when they associate themselves to Unergia.

Unergia aims to create communities founded on transparency and trust with the aim of democratizing power and giving the authority back to the people. Unergia does this by crowdfunding solar projects for residential, commercial and academic institutions. As consumers use the electricity sourced by renewable sources, they pay lesser than they did with existing electricity grid and the majority of the monthly electricity bill goes back to the crowd funders. Unergia takes a small cut from these revenue channels.

Blockchain

The Blockchain network is integral to the trust system of Unergia. The network ensures that the various stakeholders (crowdfunders, consumers etc.) share updated data. Blockchain cuts out the complexity and costs. In the absence of the network, there would be room for mutability. Furthermore, Blockchain collects the data real-time and stores the energy values at regular intervals.

Safe and Regular Payments

Secured payments for all the stakeholders is priority for Unergia. Our sensors from the solar projects collect real time data and ensure that crowdfunders get their prerequisite payments without any delays. The payment servers are attached to the blockchain network and the data feeds itself in the system.

Longer horizons for investments

Solar panels are warranted for 5 years and will be further insured during its shell-life. This would enable investors to continue reaping returns on their investment of 10-15% for a period of 25 years. Unergia shall employ a task team to ensure high maintenance of the solar panels.

Second Hand Markets

Unergia values the freedom of its crowdfunders. The second hand markets allows to trade their positions for a certain price determined by the demand and supply forces. This would simply imply the building block of a certain solar project has been passed on from one crowdfunder to another, without having affected the project itself.
