About Us

Unergia is a reward based investment which will be trying to connect solar projects to be on the platform. It serves as a platform for small investments by various people around the globe to help in the purchase of these solar panels. This has been created to ease the process of large investments which normally make such projects financially unviable. The solar energy produced would be charged to the customer consuming it and the proceeds would be going back to the investor.
