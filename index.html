About Us

For India to grow in leaps and bounds, it is imperative that it invests in cleaner sources of energy. Unergia is helping India realise its potential in unlocking its renewable capacity. The Government set a target of generating 40 GW of renewable energy through rooftops by 2022, out of which only 2 GW has been generated up until now. The reason for the low uptake is high initial cost of investment and lack of sound financing options.

The existing energy infrastructure is centered on a few companies that wield power over electricity usage. This power leads to indiscriminate hike in energy prices at regular intervals. Unergia is an investment platform for solar projects, based on crowdfunding. We shall help rooftop providers fund their solar rooftop projects, with all the miscellaneous costs. In turn, investors of these projects will be contracted to receive financial rewards at the rate of 10-15% per year on their investment, over the life of the solar project. We are committed to ensure that everybody wins when they associate themselves to Unergia.

Unergia aims to create communities founded on transparency and trust with the aim of democratizing power and giving the authority back to the people. Unergia does this by crowdfunding solar projects for residential, commercial and academic institutions. As consumers use the electricity sourced by renewable sources, they pay lesser than they did with existing electricity grid and the majority of the monthly electricity bill goes back to the crowd funders. Unergia takes a small cut from these revenue channels.

Blockchain

The Blockchain network is integral to the trust system of Unergia. The network ensures that the various stakeholders (crowdfunders, consumers etc.) share updated data. Blockchain cuts out the complexity and costs. In the absence of the network, there would be room for mutability. Furthermore, Blockchain collects the data real-time and stores the energy values at regular intervals.

Safe and Regular Payments

Secured payments for all the stakeholders is priority for Unergia. Our sensors from the solar projects collect real time data and ensure that crowdfunders get their prerequisite payments without any delays. The payment servers are attached to the blockchain network and the data feeds itself in the system.

App- Based interface

Potential investors can surf through the different projects that require to be crowdfunded, on the app-based interface of Unergia. They can choose projects based on personal parameters and invest accordingly. The users’ personal page that opens up on login also contains private information regarding the payments and projects.

Second Hand Markets

Unergia values the freedom of its crowdfunders. The second hand markets allows to trade their positions for a certain price determined by the demand and supply forces. This would simply imply the building block of a certain solar project has been passed on from one crowdfunder to another, without having affected the project itself.

Fully Secured Panels

Unergia ensures solar panel retailers/manufacturers provides fully insured and warrantied equipment. The after-sale service and maintenance will leave no stone unturned to ensure the projects can sustain themselves for a period of 20-25 years. The security is as important for us, as it is for any other stakeholder.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does second hand market work?

The second hand market is to enable people to trade their financial positions for a price determined by the demand and supply. The financial position can be sold any time. It is being developed at the moment.

When will the project start?

The project starts when the entire funding requirement is met. Crowdfunders can check the status any time through the app. Once the target is reached, the installation is done within a week.

Am I guaranteed a minimum return?

Yes, crowdfunders are guaranteed a minimum return, which is dependent on the project and the amount of investment.

How are returns calculated?

Unergia approaches consumers that have been using the conventional power. Once they agree and their project is crowdfunded, Unergia strikes a deal through Purchasing Power Agreement (PPA) to buy the solar energy at a rate sufficiently above the cost of producing the solar energy, but significantly below the conventional power. Depending on the project, Unergia guarantees a yearly return to the consumer within the range promised. For more information, click this link.

What is blockchain and how does Unergia use it?

In the context of Unergia, blockchain is a transparent database that allows multiple stakeholders to receive immutable information. Time sensitive data is transmitted periodically, where intermediaries add cost and complexity. Unergia complements the IoT features of the solar projects with blockchain to ensure that data regarding electricity usage, payment calculations and transmissions, maintenance work and payments, are not only responsibly stored but also kept transparent.
There is no involvement of cryptocurrencies in the entirety of Unergia.

What happens if the project is damaged or destroyed?

Unergia only partners with solar project retailers and manufacturers that can insure and warrant the projects for a period of 25 years. The insurance costs are included in the project costs that are to be crowdfunded.
